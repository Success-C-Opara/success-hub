SEO Basics for Wix Websites

Learn how to make your Wix site visible on Google with these beginner-friendly tips.

1. Adding Keywords

Start with keyword research. Use tools like Google Keyword Planner or Ubersuggest. Add your main keywords naturally into your page titles, headings, and content.

2. Optimizing Titles & Meta Tags

Wix lets you edit your pageâ€™s SEO (title tag and meta description) under Page Settings. Make sure each page has a unique title and meta description with relevant keywords.

3. Mobile Optimization

Most visitors use mobile devices. Wix automatically creates a mobile-friendly version of your site, but check the mobile editor to ensure everything looks good.

4. Using Wix SEO Wiz Tool

Wix includes an SEO Wiz tool that guides you step-by-step through optimizing your website. It creates a personalized SEO plan and helps you implement it easily.

5. Submitting to Google Search Console

After publishing, connect your Wix site to Google Search Console. This tells Google your site exists and speeds up indexing.
