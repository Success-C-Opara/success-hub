1. Yoast SEO

A drag-and-drop page builder that lets you design beautiful websites without coding.

3. WooCommerce

The go-to plugin for creating an online store with WordPress. Perfect for eCommerce businesses.

4. WPForms

Easy form builder for contact forms, surveys, and more. Drag-and-drop interface makes setup simple.

5. Jetpack

A multipurpose plugin that adds security, backups, site analytics, and performance improvements.

6. Rank Math

An alternative to Yoast SEO, offering advanced keyword optimization and schema markup tools.

7. UpdraftPlus

A powerful backup plugin that lets you store backups on Google Drive, Dropbox, or Amazon S3.

8. WP Rocket

A caching plugin that improves loading speed, which is crucial for SEO and user experience.

9. Akismet

A spam protection plugin that automatically filters out spam comments.

10. MonsterInsights

Connects Google Analytics to your WordPress dashboard, so you can track traffic and user behavior.
